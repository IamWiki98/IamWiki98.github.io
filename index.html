About Me

Hello, my name is Viktor. From a young age I have had a strong interest in Mathematics. After studying in Mathematics focused high school, I found myself enthused by Game Programming! My journey as a programmer began with my studies at Breda University of Applied Sciences. There, I was a part of the International Game Architecture and Design (IGAD) program. I spend time surrounded by experienced game developers (producers, designers, artists, and programmers) who taught me how to achieve my goals. My education at BUas began by developing my C++ skills. Later on, I worked with multiple multidisciplinary teams using Unreal Engine 4, Unity, and Godot or building engines with other programmers.

Apart from Game Development, I regularly play guitar or piano. I view myself as an outgoing person as I tend to smile and laugh a lot around people. I try to maintain a healthy lifestyle by exercising outside. I enjoy playing video games, and I also love going on adventures with friends.
