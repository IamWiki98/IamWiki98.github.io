About Me

Hello! I am Viktor. I have had a strong interest in Mathematics from a young age. After studying in a Mathematics Highschool, I found myself enthused by what is called Game Programming! My journey as a programmer started by beginning my studies at Breda University of Applied Sciences. There, I was a part of the International Game Architecture and Design (IGAD) program. I found myself surrounded by experienced game developers (producers, designers, artists, and programmers) who taught me how to achieve my goals. My education at BUas began by developing my C++ skills. Later on, I worked with multiple multidisciplinary teams using Unreal Engine 4, Unity, and Godot or building engines with other programmers.

Apart from Game Development, I regularly play musical instruments such as a guitar or piano. I view myself as an outgoing person as I tend to smile and laugh a lot around people. I try to maintain a healthy lifestyle by exercising outside. I enjoy playing video games, and I also love going out on adventures with friends.
